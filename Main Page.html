Ngee Ann Polytechnic

Ambassadors

Ngee Ann Polytechnic Ambassadors are the marketing arm of Ngee Ann Poly. Some of our key flagship events includes Ngee Ann's very own RED Camp, Open House and Youth Leaders Camp. We also do outreach events by going to different Secondary Schools and share about the different aspects of Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

RED Camp

Red Camp is a camp started for 'O' Level and ITE students to know more about Ngee Ann and its courses. This Camp takes place every year after 'O' Level period has ended and takes place across a span of 2-3 days. It is fille with exciting programmes such as Course Discoveries hosted by the respective academic schools for students to find out more about their favourite courses. As Ambassadors, some of the event details we help plan includes being Student Leaders (SLs) for the students as well as planning the logistics required, the program flows and the social media posts to promote the event.

Open House

NP's Open House is another one of Ambassador's flagship events with it being the largest scale. Open house is open to the genral public for anyone who wishes to visit Ngee Ann and to get to know us more. From Secondary 1 students to parents and even teachers. As Amabassadors we help with the event details such as the school tours for the public, answering queries from the public as well as doing social media promotion for the event itself.
